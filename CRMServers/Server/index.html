About Petsfort

Welcome to Petsfort (“we”, “us”, “our”). Petsfort is a cart-tracker application for dog accessory sales, designed to work fully offline within Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. We do not process payments or offer direct e‑commerce through the app. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and details what data, if any, we collect and how we handle it. This policy governs data collected through the Petsfort app and our website at petsfort.in. We adhere to the Google Play Developer Program Policies, including User Data and Data Safety requirements, and follow applicable regional data protection laws.
