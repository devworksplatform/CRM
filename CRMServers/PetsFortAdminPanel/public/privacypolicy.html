1. Introduction

Welcome to Petsfort (“we”, “us”, “our”). Petsfort is a cart-tracker application for dog accessory sales, designed to work fully offline within Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. We do not process payments or offer direct e‑commerce through the app. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and details what data, if any, we collect and how we handle it.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information that could identify an individual.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on data.
  • App: The Petsfort Android application (package com.petsfort.app).
  • Data Safety: As defined by Google Play Developer Program policies.

3. Scope & Compliance

This policy governs data collected through the Petsfort app and our website at petsfort.in. We adhere to the Google Play Developer Program Policies, including User Data and Data Safety requirements, and follow applicable regional data protection laws.

4. Information We Collect

Petsfort collects only the following data to facilitate app functionality and internal tracking:

  • User Authentication Data: Username and password for authorized staff logins. Accounts are provisioned by our administrators; no self‑registration.
  • Cart & Billing Data: Product names, quantities, pricing details used to generate in‑app bills.
  • Device & App Metrics: Device model, OS version, app version for troubleshooting only.

5. Information We Do Not Collect

Petsfort does not collect or transmit any personal or sensitive information, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers.
  • Location data (GPS, network, Bluetooth).
  • Financial or payment info.
  • Sensitive personal data (health, biometric, demographic).
  • Behavioral analytics or marketing identifiers.

6. App Permissions

Petsfort only requests the following permission:

  • INTERNET (android.permission.INTERNET) – used solely for optional catalog updates and administrative login checks. All core functions run offline.

No other permissions (camera, storage, location, microphone, contacts) are requested.

7. Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database on the user’s device. Authorized staff may manually sync data to our secure server via HTTPS for backup. Backups are retained only as long as necessary for business continuity and deleted thereafter.

8. Security Measures

  • AES-256 encryption for local data storage.
  • TLS 1.3 for any data transmitted during manual sync.
  • Access controls for server backups limited to designated staff.

9. Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Investigate and contain the incident promptly.
  • Notify affected staff via email and our official communication channels.
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law.

10. Third-Party Services & Libraries

Petsfort uses only open-source libraries for UI (Jetpack Compose) and local database (SQLite). We do not embed any analytics, advertising, or social media SDKs. No automatic data sharing with third parties.

11. GDPR & Other International Laws

Although Petsfort does not collect personal data, we comply with GDPR principles:

  • Data minimization: only necessary data is processed.
  • No processing of user data without consent.
  • Data retention limited to business needs.

12. CCPA & California Privacy Rights

As we do not collect personal information, CCPA rights do not apply. However, California residents may request information on what data, if any, we hold and request its deletion via the contact details below.

13. Children’s Privacy

Petsfort is intended for staff over 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor’s data has been collected, please contact us to delete it.

14. Google Play Data Safety Declaration

  • Data Collected: None.
  • Data Shared: None.
  • Security: Encryption at rest and in transit.
  • User Control: Clear local data via Android Settings.

15. Website Privacy & Cookies

Our website at petsfort.in is informational only and does not use cookies or track visitors. We collect no web analytics.

16. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

This policy is governed by Indian law. Disputes will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

17. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.
